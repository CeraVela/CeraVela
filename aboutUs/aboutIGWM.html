Investors Group Wealth Management, or IG Wealth Management, is an organization that gives their client financial advice in numerous areas such as investments, retirements, home ownership, and many more.

IG Wealth Management provides their clients with a personal advisor to help them allocate their money correctly and plan their future. Every decision is made with integrity, responsibility, and respect for their people, community, and environment.

They have graciously chosen to sponsor Junior Achievement and provide our company, CeraVela, with all the materials necessary to start up our own business. Without them, we would not have the chance to provide the world with out quality candles.
