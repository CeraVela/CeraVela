Sales Department

The Sales Department is in charge of monitoring and tracking any products that may have been sold. They are also responsible for tracking inventory. The Sales Department teaches us how to sell our products and do sales pitches to sell our products. They are essential to any sales that we make.

Marketing Department

The Marketing Department is responsible for making our company known to the general public. They make all our advertisements and posts on Instagram using photo editting and design software. The reason why people know about our company is because of the Marketing Department. They are the reason why our company is known to people.

Human Resources Department

The Human Resources Department is responsible for ensuring that everything is running smoothly in our company relationship wise. If there are any conflicts between two parties, the Human Resources Department helps us resolve that conflict. Once in a while, they improve our employee moral by bringing in food or drinks and giving us time to bond. They allow our company to run smoothly.

Production Department

The Product Department is responsible for the making of our products. They ensure that our products are at the highest quality for our valued customers. They have spent a lot of time researching how to make our product top-notch and have patiently taught us what they have learned. They are crucial to ensuring that our product is at the quality that you deserve.

Finance Department

The Finanace Department is responsible for managing the company's money. They track the company's costs, expenses, and revenue to ensure that we don't lose track of any money. They make sure that the money in the company is properly allocated and they manage our budget efficiently The Finanace Department spends lots of time diligently number-crunching to confirm that the numbers that they have are precise. They are vital to keeping the company's funds in check.

Technology Department

The Technology Department is responsible for all things technology related. They are responsible for setting up and managing a method for communication for our company. They also create, monitor, and manage the company's website which is used as a way to market, and eventually sell our products. The Technology Department helps company members with any problems they may have when using technology. They are necessary for the use of technology within our company.
