Perfect Valentine's Day Gift

A flower and a candle are both common items on a table during date night. Combine the two and you get our newest product, FloraVela, especially made for Valentine's Day. This flower-shaped candle is a perfect gift to give as a token of love and gratitude for the ones that mean the most to you.

Aromatic

Our candles come with a unique set of aromas such as vanilla, rose, and many more. The aroma changes the atmosphere of the room and can make you feel less tense. Our candles are designed to give off a scent that relieves stress and anxiety so it is perfect for relaxing with a loved one.

Environmentally Sustainable

With climate change becoming more and more of an issue, companies must take responsibility to ensure that their business does not have a negative impact on the environment. Here at CeraVela, we are well aware of the social responsibility that we have. We have made sure that the materials that we are using are eco-friendly. The packaging that we use is recyclable which help reduce waste. The material is also locally sourced which allows us to reduce our carbon footprint.

Handcrafted

Each candle is made by each of our company members. CeraVela works hard to give you the top quality candles that you deserve. With love and care, each candle is made and perfected so that we can give you a product that exceeds your expectations.
